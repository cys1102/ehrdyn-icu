# Explicit non-claims and limitations

- EHR results are development benchmark evidence; no genuinely untouched confirmatory role exists.
- No task completes the full retrospective scoring contract. No EHR policy value, ranking, or winner is reported.
- Known-value true return comes from constructed mechanisms and does not validate a causal action-response model for EHR data.
- Factual action-information differences are predictive associations.
- Sepsis is an AI-Clinician-aligned K25 scaffold with local parameterization, not an exact cohort/reward reproduction.
- The anchor-relative 90-day sepsis outcome is unavailable under the repaired contract.
- The reward functions are frozen computational objectives and sensitivity choices.
- Respiratory and shock known-value rewards are task specific; sepsis SOFA/lactate weights are not transferred.
- AF/flutter remains world-model-only. AKI and heart failure use separate decision-task extensions for known-value policy comparison.
- No external-site or methodological-generalization result is available.
- Model-free adapters retain their fidelity labels and are not official exact implementations.
- Prediction accuracy does not establish counterfactual fidelity or retrospective policy improvement.
- The adaptive exact-finite results evaluate known constructed mechanisms; historical monotone environments are evaluator sensitivities only.
- Independent credentialed reconstruction remains pending and is a clean-clone aggregate reproducibility check, not external validation.
