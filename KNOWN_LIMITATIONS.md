# Known Limitations

## KDD-RV successor release candidate

- `KDD-RV-SUCCESSOR-RC1` is local and unpublished; it must not be cited as a
  tagged release or independent reproduction.
- The exact RV01R/RV02R backend is hash-pinned and locally invokable, but is
  not vendored into this repository. A self-contained public source archive is
  still a release gate.
- The frozen task table has disclosed contract/code discrepancies: validation
  does not select among action alternatives, respiratory realizes three action
  classes and excludes missing-PEEP evaluator transitions, and the stated AKI
  chronic/pre-existing-RRT exclusion lacks a separate executable receipt.
- Five qualified clinical task reviews, an independent credentialed
  construction receipt, final-manuscript parity, institutional determination,
  and successor-specific subgroup/fairness auditing remain pending.
- The portable evaluator scores supplied predictions. It cannot prove from
  prediction values alone that an external producer respected recursion; a
  conditional-recursive submission must acknowledge the frozen producer
  contract and retain its source provenance.
- Direct-identifier/restricted-artifact scans and aggregate subject/cell floors
  reduce obvious disclosure risk but do not prove zero re-identification risk.

## Historical KDD089 benchmark

- MIMIC-IV access is credentialed and no data are redistributed.
- No non-sepsis task passes every sepsis-relative axis.
- Respiratory policy evaluation is excluded after action-observation repair.
- AKI and HF have no rich repeated-action contract.
- Frozen common-family uncertainty lacks Cov80, CRPS, IntervalScore90, risk-coverage, and five-member variance decomposition aggregates.
- Policy/OPE/FQE evidence is diagnostic-only and confounded by indication.
- Exact KDD078 target-policy probability surfaces are unavailable. OPE rows are quarantined from public leaderboard submissions.
- Independent ICU/cardiology adjudication of the seven compact paper contracts remains pending.
- The credentialed construction path is public, but independent parity reproduction requires an authorized MIMIC-IV v3.1 environment.
- Medication-family axes use recorded exposure with overlap-prorated amounts; they are not cross-drug dose-equivalent intensities, and local units still require credentialed and clinical review.
- The respiratory contract retains its historical nominal five-bin identifier, but duplicate train quantiles yield empirical `K=4`; reports must state both facts.
