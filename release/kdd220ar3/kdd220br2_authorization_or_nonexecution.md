# KDD220BR2 authorization

KDD220BR2 is authorized only from the exact immutable commit containing the
complete KDD220AR3 decision. It must use a fresh clean clone, official MIMIC-IV
v3.1 files through a private local argument, the frozen 250,000-row chunk size,
and `/usr/bin/time -v` or equivalent. Peak RSS must not exceed 64 GiB; the
design target is 32 GiB.

Construction must finish before KDD152 reference access. KDD220BR2 may not
repair code, alter chunk size or contracts after observing results, train a
model, or trigger a scientific rerun. Only its own complete exact or resolved
metadata-only decision may authorize a public handoff tag.
