# Migration from v1.3.0 to v2.0.0

Version 1.3.0 remains the immutable constructed-environment recursive entrant
release. Version 2.0.0 adds a separate credentialed-EHR component scorer and
adopts the explicitly versioned canonical-v2 five-task constructor lineage.

The migration does not claim that the public constructor reproduces the
superseded historical aggregate counts. KDD220M1 froze the v2 task contract,
KDD220M2R2 completed the scientific refresh but retained its original privacy
stop, and KDD220M2R2A separately authorized the immutable scientific surface
after a reporting-only privacy adjudication. The original stop remains part of
the provenance and is not renamed.

Mixed v1/v2 submissions are rejected. Every v2 result must declare both
`ehrdyn-icu-canonical-v2.0.0` and `ehr-component-scorer-v2.0.0`.

The major version does not retroactively change v1.3.0 results, expand the
real-EHR claim boundary, or provide planning, direct return, known policy
value, causal effects, treatment recommendations, or clinical utility.
